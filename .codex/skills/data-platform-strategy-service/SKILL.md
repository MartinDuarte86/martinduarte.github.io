# Data Platform Strategy Service

Use this skill for `Data Platform Strategy`.

- Strategy service for the target data platform.
- Goal: define target vision, required capabilities, technology criteria, operating model, and staged roadmap.
- Preserve the pain points: no platform vision, tool sprawl, unclear technology direction, weak support for analytics or ML.
- Preserve deliverables: strategy document, target platform vision, technology guidelines, operating model, staged roadmap, recommendations for batch, streaming, analytics, and governance.
- Preserve benefits: strategic clarity, lower investment risk, alignment across business, architecture, and operations.
